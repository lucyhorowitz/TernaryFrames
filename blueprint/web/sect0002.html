A closed set is a set of positions \(X\) satisfying \(X^{\perp \perp } = X\). The type ClosedSet \(L\) requires only a bare IncoherenceSpace; the shape of closed sets varies with additional structural conditions.

  • IncoherenceSpace.ClosedSet.instLattice

Under Exchange, ClosedSet \(L\) is a lattice:

  • \(X \sqcap Y = X \cap Y\) (intersection of closed sets is closed).

  • \(X \sqcup Y = (X \cup Y)^{\perp \perp }\) (the closure of the union).

  • \(\top = \mathcal{U}\) (the universe, always closed under Exchange).

  • IncoherenceSpace.ClosedSet.instComplementedLattice

Under Containment, ClosedSet \(L\) is a complemented lattice with:

  • \(\bot = \mathcal{I}\) (the incoherence set).

  • The complement of \(X\) is \(X^\perp \).

  • \(X \sqcap X^\perp = \bot \) and \(X \sqcup X^\perp = \top \).

Under Containment, the lattice of \({\perp \perp }\)-closed sets is distributive.

The proof uses Zorn’s lemma to extend coherent sets of moves to maximal ones, then injects the lattice into a power-set Boolean algebra.
